Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2026 · Applies to this website and the game "Wonderkid — A Football Life"

The short version

Wonderkid has no accounts, no sign-ups, and collects no personal information. Your game save lives in your own browser. If ads are shown on this site, they are served by Google AdSense, which uses cookies as described below.

Game saves

Your career progress is stored using your browser's local storage, on your own device. It never leaves your device and we cannot see it. Clearing your browser data will delete your save.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more detail on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, see Google's policy.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used — things like which pages are visited, roughly where visitors are from, and what devices they use. Google Analytics sets cookies and collects usage data on Google's behalf; we see only aggregated statistics, never who you are. You can opt out with the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, or by blocking cookies in your browser. See how Google uses data from partner sites.

Children

The game is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
